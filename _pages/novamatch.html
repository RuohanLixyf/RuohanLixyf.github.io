Research Pipeline

NovaMatch

Trajectory Processing Pipeline for Planning Insight

NovaMatch is a research-oriented pipeline that converts raw mobility trajectories into planning-network semantics and generates comprehensive analytics on OD patterns, route similarity, and access/egress connector usage.

Large-scale Data Wejo / VDOT trajectories and planning datasets
Planning Semantics Translate to planning network & TAZ system
Rich Analytics OD, similarity, connector usage and more
Research Driven Built for reproducible experiments & papers
